Buck Converter

Overview

Asynchronous buck converter designed around the LM22676, stepping down 24V to 5-7.5V at 1.5A continuous output.

Key Specs

  • IC: LM22676MRE (Texas Instruments)
  • Input voltage: 24V
  • Output voltage: 5-7.5V, set by trimmer potentiometer
  • Output current: 1.5A continuous
  • Switching frequency: 500 kHz

What I Did

  • Selected components (inductor, output capacitor, diode) per LM22676 datasheet design procedure
  • Calculated inductor ripple current and output voltage ripple given load conditions and switching frequency
  • Laid out PCB following TI layout recommendations for minimizing switching noise
  • Obtained an efficiency of 90%
  • Futher efficiency and transient response characterization planned
