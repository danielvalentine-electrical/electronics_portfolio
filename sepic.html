SEPIC Converter

Overview

TI F28027-controlled SEPIC converter built on perfboard with hand-wound inductors on EFD15 N87 ferrite cores. Implements MPPT from a 10W PV panel, achieving 8.97W vs 7.93W direct — a 13% improvement.

Key Specs

  • Microcontroller: TI F28027 C2000 LaunchPad
  • Gate driver: MCP1407
  • L1: 37 turns, 220 µH | L2: 23 turns, 153 µH
  • Current-sense transformer: 1:36 ratio, 110Ω burden
  • MPP output: 8.97W at D=50%

What I Did

  • Hand-wound inductors on EFD15/8/5 N87 ferrite cores
  • Designed bifilar current-sense transformer for inductor current measurement
  • Characterized PV panel I-V curve and validated against LTspice single-diode model
  • Debugged gate driver and output diode issues during bring-up
  • Compared averaged switch model simulation vs. experimental waveforms
